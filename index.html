Navi Movement Videos for Realistic MuJoCo Simulation

A motion-reference library for reproducing Navi's gait, posture, timing, balance, recovery, and expressive behavior in MuJoCo. Every item below is an actual H.264 MP4 video, cut to the commanded motion and labeled with its exact SDK command.
